saito
Saito contains a built-in archiving service.

Since browsers cannot store gigabytes of data in local memory, Saito contains a built-in protocol for transmitting data to services in the network. To get started, open your email client, select the "archive" module and follow the instructions on-screen. You can also enable and change archival servers by editing your configuration file: who and what you rely upon to store your data depends entirely on you.
